Newmont Boddington Gold Request to Access Security Retricted Area

Terms and Condition of Entry into Security Restricted Areas

I understand that access to the Security Restricted Areas to which I have requested access in this document is limited to authorised persons and subject to NGB Security Procedures. As a condition of access to the Security Restricted Areas (SRA):

  • I have submitted a National Police History Check prior to authorisation being granted to enter NGB Site and NGB SRA.

  • I consent to NGB sharing details regarding my employment with the WAPOL and other law enforcement agencies from time to time.

  • I understand that NGB has the right to refuse entry into a SRA or revoke previously granted access at any time and that NGB is under no obligation to provide the reasons for such a refusal.

  • I consent to having my person and / or property, goods and materials taken into a SRA searched whilst entering and/or leaving the area and at any time whilst in the SRA. I understand that refusal to allow such searches may result in disciplinary action including termination of my employment and possible referral to law enforcement agencies for further investigation.

  • I understand that the materials that can be taken into a SRA are restricted and understand that NGB has the right to restrict the taking in of certain materials or may seal those materials before they can be taken in.

  • I consent to comply with all NGB Security Procedures related to SRA. I understand that refusal to allow such searches may result in disciplinary action including termination of my employment and possible referral to the law enforcement agencies for further investigation.

  • I understand that unauthorised removal of gold bearing ore or other materials in any form or the concealment of gold bearing or other materials in any form shall result in termination of my employment and referral to law enforcement agencies for possible prosecution.
